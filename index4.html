Feedback Grading Scale

  • <=0 and <=5 : Un-Satisfactory
  • >6 and <=7 : Satisfactory
  • >8 and <=9 : Good
  • >5 and <=6 : Cause of Concern
  • >7 and <=8 : Good
  • >9 and <=10 : Excellent

Questions To Grade For Each Subject

Q1. Compotent, knowledgeable in the subject ?
Q2. Comes well prepared to the class. Lectures are well organised. Coverage of Syllabus, Quality of handouts ?
Q3. Teaching style is good and clear, Explainations are given, Ability to clear doubts. Communication Skills ?
Q4. Encourage students to express their ideas, dialogues and questions in classroom ?
Q5. Assignments are well planned, relevent and integrated with teaching ?
Q6. Very fair and objective in assesment. Evaluation of class tests and Discussions ?
Q7. Enthusiastic about teaching the subjects ?
Q8. Accessible to students for individual or group discussion outside the class hours, Personal intrests ?
Q9. Is a good Role Model, Punctualty, Regularty and Real Life Application ?
Q10. Has Leadership Qualities, Class Control ?
